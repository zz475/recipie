Instructions

  1. It is needed for a 3 liter saucepan:
  2. Pork with bone - 400 gr.
  3. White cabbage - 180 gr.
  4. Onion - 100 gr.
  5. Beetroot - 150 gr.
  6. Potatoes - 300 gr.
  7. Carrot - 80 gr.
  8. Bell pepper - 120 gr.
  9. Garlic - 5 gr.
  10. Tomato paste - 40 gr.
  11. Ground coriander - a pinch.
  12. Bay leaf - 2 pcs.
  13. Black pepper, hot red pepper pepper - a pinch.
  14. Sunflower oil - 60 ml.
  15. Sugar - 5 grams.
  16. Dill, sour cream.
  1. Finely chop the onion, cut the peeled beetroot, carrot, bell pepper (pulp) into strips.
  2. Dice the potato tubers.
  3. Fry the beetroot slices for 5 minutes. Then add the onions, carrots and peppers (if desired, they can be fried separately, but then transferred to the beets).
  4. Dilute the tomato paste with a little broth and pour it over the vegetables. Simmer until the beets are tender.
  5. Remove the cooked meat from the pan. Divide into small portions. Put the potatoes instead of the meat. Cook for 15-20 minutes until the potatoes are soft.
  6. Chop the cabbage and add to the soup.
  7. After 5 minutes, transfer the dressing and chopped garlic from the pan.
  8. Salt the dish and add sugar. Boil the borscht, then turn it off and let it brew.
